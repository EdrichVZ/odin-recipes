Crusty Bread Pizza

Description:

Easy as pie. Prepare your own homegrown South African pizza using only 5 ingredients!

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium size french loaf or ciabatta
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic & Herb Seasoning
  • Roasted Red Pepper Pesto or Sun-dried Tomato Pesto
  • 1 cup (250ml) grated cheese like Mozzarella, cheddar, or a mixture of both

Steps:

  1. Adjust the oven rack one slot above the middle and preheat the oven to 200 Â°C.
  2. Slice the bread in half, lengthways. Place on a baking sheet. Drizzle and brush cut sides with olive oil and sprinkle lightly with Garlic & Herb Seasoning.
  3. Bake in the oven for 7 minutes. Remove baking sheet from the oven and spread cut sides generously with the Pesto of your choice. Top with cheese.
  4. Return to the oven and cook for a final 10 minutes or until the cheese is bubbling.

Variations:

Add sliced cooked chicken, sliced ham, slices of salami or tomato before topping the pizzas with cheese.
